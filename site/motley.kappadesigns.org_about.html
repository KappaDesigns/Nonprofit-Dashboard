Our Mission

Motley Zoo Animal Rescue is dedicated to improving the lives of animals locally, nationally and internationally; alleviating their suffering and elevating their status in society through the rescue and rehabilitation of animals in need and through the education of their community on responsible pet ownership and animal welfare, advocating the value of all animal life, in hopes to end the neglect and abuse of all animals and to end the devastating pet overpopulation through spay and neuter advocacy.

What We Do

Pets are precious, and none should be left behind

We at Motley dont operate like a regular animal shelter. Our main objective is to assist animals by:

  1. Accepting pets the shelters won't
  2. Accepting owner surrenders
  3. consuling and advising owners on acceptable practices

After excepting these animals, we send them out to our extensive network of wonderful fosters who care for these pets, this allows us to achieve these amazing statistics.

  • 99% live release rate
  • 2100 pets rescued and re-housed
  • accepting dog, cats and other animals

Sponsors

Shelter Animals Count

We proudly participate in Maddie’s Fund, “Shelter Animals Count” Program. Thanks, Maddie!

All The Best Pet Care

All the Best Pet Care has been a great supporter of Motley Zoo. They’ve proudly donated much needed quality foods for our pets, host adoption events at their stores and help raise much needed funds for veterinary care. We’re very grateful for the support of this great, locally owned company!

Petco Foundation

The Petco Foundation has been key in helping us over the years and we couldn’t be more grateful. Not only have they made tackling making spays and neuters possible for more pets, but their grants have helped us cover the $15,000 each month we spend in vet bills.

Pet Finder

Petfinder.com is a great resource for rescues, shelters and pet seekers alike and their foundation has graciously donated funding, vaccines, Thundershirts and more, to our rescue to help make our pets happier, healthier and ready for adoption!

Petsmart Charities

We are grateful to the support of PetSmart Charities which along with Petsmart stores, offers great opportunities and grants to Motley Zoo!

Washington Federation Of Animal Care

jme, our Executive Director, is a member of the Board of The Washington Federation and we are proud to help expand the humane animal welfare movement within Washington State.
